Zaiyi Chen ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Zaiyi Chen. I received my Ph.D (and B.E) degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China(USTC) in 2019 (2012). My advisor is Enhong Chen, and my co-advisor is Linli Xu. I joined Prof. Tianbao Yang's group as a visiting scholar, working on first-order stochastic optimization, from Oct. 2016 to Oct. 2017. In 2019, I joined Cainiao Network. Now I am working on practical approximation algorithms in logistics and finance as a senior algorithm expert.
